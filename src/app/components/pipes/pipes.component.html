In Angular, pipes are used to transform data in templates, they don't change the original content, they just transform the content that is displayed in view. Pipes can be pure and impure.

Pure Pipes

  • Pure pipes are called only when input changes.
  • Pure pipes are stateless, and are highly optimized.

    • Example: lowercase, uppercase, titlecase, date, currency, decimal, percent, etc..

Impure Pipes

  • Impure pipes are called on every change detection cycle, whether the input is changed or not.
  • Then can be statefule or have side effects.

    • Example: async, slice and json
