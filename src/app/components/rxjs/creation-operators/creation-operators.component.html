Creation operators

of() - used to convert values into observable

from() - used to convert promise or arrays into observable

interval() - used to create an observable that emits sequence of values at regular intervals.

timer(initialDelay, period(intervalDelay)) - used to crate an observable that emits values after a delay like setTimeout, or emits repeatedly after an initial delay like (setTimeout + setInterval)

range(start, count) - it creats an observable that emits a sequence of numbers in order.
